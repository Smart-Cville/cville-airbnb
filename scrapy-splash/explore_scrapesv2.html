Charlottesville short-term rental summary

Nathan Day

2020-03-09

Total listings in Charlottesville

Results from map search of the Chalottesville area on Airbnb and VRBO. After results were collected a geo-filter was performed to select only listings within the city limits.

Then a zoning geo-filter was applied to identify properties as “by-right” or “restricted” for short-term rental. Zoning codes considered as “by-right” are: B-1,B-1C,B-1H,B-2,B-2H,B-3,B-3H,CC,CD,CH,D,DE,DN,HS,HW,NCC,SS,URB,WME,WMW,WSD.

Airbnb listings available Monday-Friday

Three separate 1-week (Monday - Friday) searches for the Charlottesville area were performed on AirBnB. The first full calendar week in each month was used:

  • October 5-9
  • November 2-6
  • December 7-11
